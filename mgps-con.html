POLICIES AND PROCEDURES & STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (SOPS)


Every healthcare facility must have an operating policy that is effective for medical gas pipeline systems. Your operational policy will be created by us to satisfy your demands as well as the needs of your stakeholders. By creating a policy and procedure document that includes MGPS components, emergency procedures, etc. for you to review and sign off, we make it easier to put the policy into practise.

We will develop clear SOPs for medical gas related supply chain processes including forecasting, ordering, and distribution. SOPs for biomedical engineering personnel relating to equipment management and maintenance will also be developed. SOPs will be executed consistently throughout the supply chain, and managers will be assigned to monitor process quality and to optimize it as needed.


CYLINDER STORAGE AND OPTIMISATION


The majority of cylinders are leased from centralised gas providers that do not offer maintenance of the cylinders and other components. Supply concerns are introduced since regular refilling necessitates transportation, which might be impractical in terms of logistics, cost, and security. Furthermore, broken or improperly fitting regulators can cause cylinders to leak up to 50% of their contents. A delicate balance must be struck between daily consumption, emergency stock, cylinder safety, and rental prices in order to maintain the proper quantity of cylinders. Our cylinder optimization service examines each of these factors and makes suggestions for having the appropriate stock, properly kept in the appropriate location, and the proper procedure in place to be able to respond to emergencies. We'll examine the following components:

The level of cylinder usage

The storage areas

Ordering, administration and delivery

Cylinder type, quantity and use on wards

Cylinder safety, security and compliance of accommodation

Cylinder storage and segregation

Racking and supports
