MGPS DESIGN, DRAWINGS & SPECIFICATION REQUIREMENTS



Thorough planning is necessary to achieve the goals of the MGPS project, thus it is crucial to collect enough specific data at the beginning of the design phase. Together with the pertinent parties, we will develop clear guidelines for the project's scope, purpose, needed standards, and interpretation of department designations.

DRAWING SERVICES



To handle your MGPS safely and effectively, particularly during shutdowns and crises, up-to-date drawings and data are necessary.

When necessary, valves will be renumbered into one style and a complete valve log will be generated. MGPS Drawings will be prepared using industry standard blocks and symbols, contain the necessary data, and be appropriately layered. Every AVSU can be attached with engraved labelling in the Traffolyte style if necessary.

To improve and finish your drawings, we can offer some or all of the following:


Review of Site survey using the Site Plan and production of As-Fitted Drawings

Produce Schematic Drawings

Produce Isometric Drawings, Flow and Pressure drops

Apply engraved labelling to each AVSU and accessible line valve and compile a valve log


DESIGN & SPECIFICATIONS


We provide validation of design services to ensure your system meets all the relevant standards and clinical needs. This can include some or all, but is not limited to the following:

Review of Site survey using the Site Plan for siting of Cryogenic Plant or new Plant

Review of Site survey using the Site Plan for Compound, Plant and Manifold room requirements

MGPS flow calculations and pipe sizing

Source equipment sizing and specification

Main, Ready to use and Local cylinder storage

Installations and maintenance requirements

ESSENTIAL MAINTENANCE & PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE SERVICE



To enhance the effectiveness of the MGPS, we will create standard operating procedures (SOPs) and precise protocols that clearly define the roles and duties of all relevant MGPS Stakeholders. In order to encourage adherence to and implementation of the necessary planned preventative maintenance programmes, we will work with your healthcare facility.

SOPs for biomedical engineering personnel relating to equipment management and maintenance will also be developed and managers will be tasked to monitor process quality and to optimize it as needed.

Additionally, healthcare institutions will have access to manuals and guidelines, including those specific to individual items, on how to use and maintain medical gas equipment. Facilities requiring more complicated repairs will be supported with readily available, experienced technical help that adheres to pre-approved SLAs. The improvement of these practises will aid in preventing protracted maintenance delays.



HEALTH INFORMATION SYSTEMS


Medical gas supply must be uninterrupted at service delivery locations in order for healthcare facilities to operate safely. By maintaining the purchase of enough and suitable amounts, our efficient and sustainable supply chain system for medical gases guarantees that the demand for medical gases does not exceed the supply that is currently available. By applying data-driven quantification of demands, we will be able to do away with supply stock-outs, administrative red tape, and unreliable suppliers.

By explicitly defining actions and duties within the SOPs to offer unambiguous arrangements governing the movement and transportation of full or empty cylinders, we develop procedures to reinforce the distribution systems of medicinal gases within facilities.

Our MGPS systems depend on a powerful Logistics Management Information System (LMIS), which has an automated inventory control and a reliable supply chain system.
